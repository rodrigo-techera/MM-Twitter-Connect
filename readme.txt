== Description ==

This Plugin Allow you to have Twitter Social Login in your site.

= Special Settings =

A section is added to your Settings menu, allowing you to configure your credentials and the styles for the button.

== Installation ==

1. Download MM Twitter Connect.
1. Upload the 'mm-twitter-connect' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
1. Activate MM Twitter Connect from your Plugins page.
1. Create a Twitter Application here <a href="https://apps.twitter.com/" target="_blank">https://apps.twitter.com/</a>.
1. Callback URL needs to be your domain.
1. Take the Client ID and Client Secret from your recent created App, and complete the fields in your settings.
